Carbonara

-Ingredients-

For 2 ppl

[For Pasta]

  • 200g of any long pasta
  • 4L of water
  • 40g of salt(1% of water)

[For Sauce]

  • 30ml of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 200g of Guanciale(pancetta or bacon can be substitutes)
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Egg york
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of black pepper
  • 50g of pecolino romano(parmigiano reggiano can be substitute)

-Process-

  • Squash garlic cloves, get rid of its peel and roots, and slice them.
  • Chop guanciale thinly so that it's gonna be easy to make it crispy.
  • Put 1 egg and 2 egg yorks in a bowl and mix it well.
  • Grate cheese on it, add salt and black pepper, and mix well again.
  • Put chopped garlic and olive oil on a pan, then heat it on with middle heat.
  • Wait until you can see bubbles around garlic cloves, slant your pan a little bit to deep-fry garlic cloves.
    • For who uses IH cooker, heat on your pan with high heat.
    Slanted your pan after you see bubbles. And put your pan back on IH cooker when the bubbles are gone.
    Repeat this process 2 to 3 times.
  • Start heating on water, then boil pasta.I recommend to boil pasta for 2 minutes shorter than what the package says.
    Preferably finish boiling pasta when sauce is done.
  • When nice smell of garlic pops up, add guanciale and grill it until it becomes crispy.
    • 　(It's up to you how much you cook guanciale but ensure that garlic cloves are not burned.)
  • Move boiled pasta into pan, also add 50ml of boiling water. Move your pan back and forth vigorously and mix pasta and sauce
    If you can, flip your pan many times to mix it better.
  • Keep mixing and feel the water you added and olive oil are mixed and getting some consistency.
  • Stop heating, wait for about 10 sec., and put pasta into the bowl. And mix immediately.
    • Make sure to mix immediately otherwise the egg would congeal.
  • Add extra salt, black pepper, and cheese if you like.

[Remarks]

If you have only one cooker in your kitchen like me, in order to let the water be as hot as possible when you start boiling pasta,
boil water first while you cut ingredients. And start heating on them after the water is boiled.
Also after the pasta is boiled, heat on pasta sauce again before adding pasta in it.
