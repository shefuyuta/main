Stewed Pork Ribs

     
   

-Ingredients-

     

For 2-3 ppl

         
           
               
  • Pork ribs...500g
    •            
  • Soy sauce(金山 seasoning sauce if possible)...150ml
    •            
  • Red wine...100ml
    •            
  • Garlic...4 cloves
    •          
  • Brown sugar...70g(8 table spoons)
    •            
  • Salt...3 pinches
  • Cilantro...8 brunches
    •            
  • Ginger...50g
    •            
  • Sesami oil...2 table spoons
  • White pepper...3 table spoons
  • Radish(option)...500g-700g
  • Water...4L
         
     

-Process-

       
         
               
  • Make small wholes on ribs with a fork, so seasonings can solk well.
  • In separated bowl, mix soy sauce, red wine, brown sugar, sesami oil, white pepper and grate garlic and ginger into the bowl.
  • Rub salt into ribs. Spread it all over the ribs.
  • Put ribs and sauce in a ziplock(can be multiple ziplocks) and close it. Take out the air as much as possible.
  • Massage ribs gently and put the ziplock in the fridge. Leave it for 2 to 3 hours.
  • Boil water in a large pod. and add everything from ziplock.
  • Cover the water with cooking sheet or aluminum foil(Drop it onto the water) and stew 4 hours over low heat.
  • Move it to container and cool it down. Put it in fredge over night.
  • Put back into a large pod and heat it on for 10 minutes.(Just to warm it up to serve.)
